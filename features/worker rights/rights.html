Pay & Leave rights: - Migrant construction workers are entitled to up to 60 days of paid hospitalisation leave annually, per the Employment Act. - Any medical leave certified by a doctor—whether from workplace injury or illness—must be paid, up to the statutory maximum (hospitalisation leave limit) . - Workers can take up to 60 calendar days of overseas leave with levy waiver annually, provided each trip is at least 7 consecutive days.
